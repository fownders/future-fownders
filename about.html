About Us

Future Fownders is a 501(c)3 youth education program based on three core pillars of Entrepreneurship, Financial Literacy and Emotional Intelligence. The program is birthed from the for-profit social enterprise Fownders, which was founded by serial entrepreneur Gerard Adams. We provide our young people the freedom to openly express and actualize their ideas through the introduction of entrepreneurial principles and mentorship, encouraging them to create, build and own their ideas as well as their personal power.

Future Fownders is an immersive program encouraging the development of the entrepreneurial mindset, the cultivation of personal growth, and the establishment of a social network of like-minded aspiring youth entrepreneurs. Our Future Fownders are developed through our in-school programming, workshop series, internship program and community outreach initiatives.

Our offering spans the realm of product-based businesses, foundational business theory as well as core intangible requirements for entrepreneurs like leadership, teamwork and communication. The result of the education is the development of valuable, practical and effective real-world skills. The program includes educational content, exclusive influencer appearances and hands-on mentorship from top business leaders. So far we have developed programs with Link Charter School, Big Picture Program at East Side High School, People’s Prep Charter, Central High School and secured a partnership for start-up capital with Real World Scholars.

Amanda Johnson

Director

Amanda was born and raised in Newark, NJ. Amanda fell in love with entrepreneurship through working in her family's business, upon exit and unclear of what direction was next, Amanda was introduced to Fownders. Having first-hand knowledge of what it is to be a kid from Newark, Amanda jumped at the opportunity to lead Future Fownders and has dedicated herself to providing this unique opportunity to students throughout Newark.

Jade Jordan

Executive Operations Assistant

Jade is Newark-born and has worked passionately as an educator and mentor with urban area youth since 2010 and believes that each child possesses a latent power to create and lead a life of fulfillment - a power which calls for the responsibility of our adults to bring that power forth and light the way. She serves Future Fownders wholeheartedly and is committed to impact - one child at a time. Jade also serves as Fownders’ Director of Community Outreach.
