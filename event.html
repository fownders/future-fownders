Future Fownders Welcomes You

Future Fownders is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit powered by Fownders that curates interactive programming with an entrepreneurial focus for middle and high school students with a concentration for youth in Newark, NJ. We are approaching our first full year of active in-school programming and we invite you to join us as we come together to highlight the impact and forward facing vision of Future Fownders!

Our inaugural gala is a celebratory campaign to garner support from individuals and corporations who believe in our purpose and seek to support the continual evolution of traditional curriculums. We provide our young people a transformative platform that promotes critical thinking, curiosity, freedom of expression, financial empowerment, and above all, personal development. Our young people complete the Future Fownders program with expanded horizons, a greater sense of their strengths and improved preparedness to be our socially conscious, goal-driven leaders of tomorrow.

We cannot do this alone. Sponsorships and donations made to the nonprofit are essential in providing Future Fownders the resources to further our mission. With your help, Future Fownders is able to increase the number of schools reached and students impacted by expanding programming and continuing to cultivate and inspire the next generation of leaders. Are you in?
