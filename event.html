Chef Elliott

Chef Elliott's love affair with food began at a young age. Not only did he quickly discover a passion for cooking, and eating; but he also discovered a deeply rooted fascination with food's ability to bring family, and friends together at the table. Many of his greatest childhood memories all have three things in common; "Family, friends, and great food".

He has spent the past 8 years honing his craft, studying at one of the top culinary schools in the nation, and cooking in several great restaurants including the world famous three Michelin starred flagship restaurant "Jean-Georges" in NYC, and he has since started his own namesake catering company, and is on a MISSION to impact people's lives through food. When he isn’t making magic in the kitchen, you can catch him either fishing, hiking, or playing with his puppy, Gussy.
